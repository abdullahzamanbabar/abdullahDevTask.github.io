Udemy is one of the largest online learning platforms for professional learning. कंपनी के अनुसार , they have 35 million registered users. An analysis by Class Central shows they’ve launched over 157,000 courses since 2010.

The pandemic has boosted the कई ऑनलाइन प्रदाताओं का भाग्य . Udemy is no exception. In 2020 alone, the company $ 123 मिलियन जुटाए and increased its valuation by over $1 billion. Overall, the company has raised ~$300 million at a valuation over $ 3 बिलियन .

वर्ग केंद्र also experienced a संगरोध बढ़ावा : of all the people who ever used Class Central, 2020 में पहली बार 40% ने ऐसा किया .

संख्याओं द्वारा udemy

Last year, we formed a सुदूर अध्ययन समूह at Class Central, and four of us took an Excel course together. To hone my एक्सेल कौशल , I decided to analyze Udemy’s active catalog.

I discovered they currently have over 157,000 courses in their catalog — a 60% increase compared to 2019. Complete analysis of Udemy’s catalog can be found here: 157K पाठ्यक्रम, 425 मीटर नामांकन: udemy की विशाल कैटलॉग को तोड़ना

Combined, these courses have gathered ~425 million enrollments and average 2700 enrollments per course. But what surprised me the most was that the median enrollment was 188. Do Udemy courses satisfy the Pareto 80/20 सिद्धांत ? I found that Udemy’s top-20% courses by enrollment account for 90% of all enrollments.
